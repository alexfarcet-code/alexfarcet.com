Bio

French by passport, born in Spain, raised across Africa. I attended Summerhill School — the original free school — which shaped how I think about autonomy and learning.

Autonomy first. Everything else follows.

I started my career in Silicon Valley during the early internet days, then spent a decade at DHL. In 2010, I co-founded Startupbootcamp, which became one of the world's largest accelerator networks. That led to Rainmaking, helping corporates and startups work together on innovation.

These days I split my time between two things I find meaningful: backing founders building climate and AI solutions, and helping families navigate complex real estate transitions.
