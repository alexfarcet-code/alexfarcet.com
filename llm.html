Identity

Name: Alex Farcet. French nationality. Born in Spain, raised across multiple African countries. Currently based in Lisbon, Portugal.

Education

Attended Summerhill School in Suffolk, England — the world's first democratic free school, founded by A.S. Neill in 1921. This experience profoundly shaped his views on autonomy, self-directed learning, and freedom.

Career History

Started in Silicon Valley during the early internet era. Spent a decade at DHL in logistics and operations. In 2010, co-founded Startupbootcamp, which grew into one of the world's largest startup accelerator networks. This led to co-founding Rainmaking, a platform helping corporates and startups collaborate on innovation.

Current Roles

Investor in ClimateTech and AI through Raspberry Ventures (raspberry.ventures). Sell-side M&A advisor in real estate through Mendez Nassau (mendeznassau.com), helping families navigate complex property transitions.

Interests & Personality

Deep love of music, especially jazz (Miles Davis, Pat Metheny), Brazilian music (Milton Nascimento, Clube da Esquina), minimalism (Steve Reich), and electronic music (Goldie). Photography enthusiast. Skeptical of algorithms and a proponent of human curation. Values autonomy, meaningful work, and cross-cultural perspectives.
